Auto Mode

Set your rules.
The bot follows them.

Three steps between your strategy and fully automatic execution. Every trade still respects your daily loss limit and stop-loss settings.

1
Set your strategy

Choose Conservative, Balanced, or Aggressive. Each preset defines the minimum security score and market cap range a token must hit before the bot executes automatically.

2
Let the scanner run

Breadbot polls DEXScreener every 5 minutes. Each new token is scored against 15 automated security checks. Only tokens that pass your preset thresholds are executed — everything else is ignored or sent for manual review.

3
Get notified

Every auto-executed trade sends an instant notification: token name, entry price, amount, and your updated position count. Your configured stop-loss is set at the same moment.

FAQ

Common questions.

Can Breadbot access or withdraw my funds?↓

No. Breadbot connects using read-and-trade-only API keys. It can see balances and place trades but cannot move funds anywhere. Withdraw and Transfer permissions are never requested during setup. Your exchange credentials remain on your own machine in an encrypted .env file that only you control.

Is it safe to let a bot trade for me?↓

In many ways safer than manual trading. Breadbot follows your rules exactly — no panic selling, no FOMO buying. Every trade respects position size limits, and the daily loss circuit breaker pauses everything automatically if losses reach your threshold. You can override, pause, or stop with one click anytime.

What happens if the market crashes while my bot is running?↓

Breadbot has two automatic protection layers. Every position includes a stop-loss that exits if the token drops to your configured level. The daily loss limit pauses all trading if total losses hit your threshold, default 5% of portfolio. You receive an instant notification the moment either triggers.

Do I need trading experience to use Breadbot?↓

No. Start with the Balanced strategy preset which has sensible defaults for everything. The dashboard explains every metric in plain English with help icons. A guided walkthrough runs the first time you open the dashboard and covers every important section in about three minutes.

How long does setup take?↓

About five minutes from purchase to first live scan. The setup wizard walks you through connecting exchange API keys and confirming risk settings. The bot confirms everything worked correctly the moment it goes live.

Can I try it without risking real money?↓

Yes. Paper trading mode runs the full scanner and shows alerts and decisions exactly as they would appear live, but no real trades are placed. Use paper mode to get comfortable with the dashboard and verify your strategy settings before switching to live trading.

What is the difference between Manual and Auto mode?↓

In Manual mode, Breadbot finds qualifying tokens, runs security checks, and sends you an alert with Buy or Skip options. Nothing executes without your approval. In Auto mode, tokens passing your configured security score and market cap thresholds execute immediately. Daily loss limits work identically in both modes.

What does the security score mean?↓

A 100-point score summarizing automated rug pull checks on every new token. It starts at 100 and loses points for specific red flags: sell tax above 5%, pausable contracts, changeable balances, concentrated ownership. Tokens scoring below 50 never reach you. Each alert shows exactly which checks deducted points.

How much do I need to invest?↓

There is no minimum required by Breadbot. Default settings are designed for $1,000–$10,000 accounts with each trade capped at 2% of portfolio. On a $1,000 account that means $20 per trade. The bot works on any account size with proportional position scaling.

Will I definitely make money?↓

No one can guarantee profits in crypto. Breadbot executes consistently, removes emotional decisions, and filters out most low-quality tokens automatically — but markets can move against any position. Start with paper trading and only invest money you can afford to lose. The 30-day money-back guarantee lets you evaluate risk-free.

Which exchanges are supported?↓

Coinbase Advanced Trade and Kraken for live trading. Solana and Base DEX pairs are monitored via DEXScreener for scanner alerts but executed through your connected CEX accounts. Yield monitoring covers Coinbase, Aave V3, Morpho, and Kraken. Additional exchange support is in development.

Can I stop, pause, or change settings anytime?↓

Yes to all three. Pause trading instantly from the Controls page — the bot stops immediately. Resume the same way. Change risk settings, strategy, and execution mode anytime from Controls, with changes taking effect on the next scan cycle. No lock-in periods.
